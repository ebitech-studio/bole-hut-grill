About Bole Hut & Grill

Welcome to Bole Hut & Grill, your trusted destination for delicious Nigerian meals in Amassoma, Bayelsa State. We are passionate about serving freshly prepared food made with quality ingredients in a clean, comfortable and friendly environment.

Our specialties include grilled fish and bole, barbecue, beans and bole, and many other tasty meals prepared with care to give every customer an unforgettable dining experience.

We also provide fast delivery services to your doorstep. In addition, we accept bookings for weddings, birthdays, naming ceremonies, parties, corporate events and other special occasions. Our professional team is always ready to serve you.

At Bole Hut & Grill, customer satisfaction is our priority. We look forward to serving you with delicious meals and excellent service.
